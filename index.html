Our Story

More Than Just a Cafe

We're the spot where friendships bloom, study groups thrive, and every meal is a memory.

🏠

Born on Campus

Zaika Cafe was founded in 2022 by students who dreamed of a space where great food meets great minds. Nestled right inside the university, we understand the student life — the late-night cravings, the budget-friendly needs, and the desire for quality.

🍳

Fresh & Affordable

Every dish is prepared fresh, daily. We source locally and keep our prices student-friendly without compromising taste.

💡

Study-Friendly

Free Wi-Fi, cozy corners, charging ports — we're designed so you can study for hours with a warm cup by your side.

🎉

Campus Events

From open mic nights to quiz competitions, we host events that bring the campus together.
